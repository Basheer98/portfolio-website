About JobTrackPro

JobTrackPro is a student project designed to help job seekers organize their applications, schedule interviews, and manage follow-ups seamlessly. Stay productive and take control of your job search process.

Features

  • Integration with Gmail to track job-related emails.
  • Interview scheduling with Google Calendar.
  • Real-time notifications for job updates and follow-ups.
  • Analytics to track application success rates.
